Project Description

We have online news from three different websites: Facebook, GooglePlus and LinkedIn. Each news has three popularity scores from the three social media websites. We also have the sentiment scores for the news title and the news headline. We want to find out, given a news, if we can build a data model to predict its popularity, based on the existing ground truth data we have. This prediction model can be used for many different applications. such as

  • advertising
  • election campaign
  • posts recommendation
  • dynamic content management
